## User Stories - Household management

### *Viewing a household*

Vivian logs in to the app. She navigates to the Dashboard. On her dashboard she can see the name and logo of household that she is a member of. She can see a list of all current members of her household. She can also see the quick stats and quick actions for the currently selected households. If Vivian is a member of multiple households she will see a list households by name at the top of her dashboard. Clicking on the name of a household will make it the currently selected household.

### *Creating a household*

Carol logs in to the app. She is automatically navigated to the Dashboard page (Home page). She can see the *Create Household* button. She clicks the create household button. She is presented with a modal dialog which contains the the input fields to create a household.

She fills in the name field and uploads an image from her device as the household logo. She then clicks the create button and the modal closes. 
At the top of the page she can now see the name of her newly created household.
And the the quick stats on the dashboard are now populated with her details.

### *Invite member to a household*

Ivan wants to add his flatmates to his household. He navigates to the Dashboard and clicks the *Invite Member* button which opens a mobile dialogue where enters the email and name of the person he wants to invite. He clicks *Send Invitation* and the system will check if the person is already a user of the app or if they are new. If the invitee is already an app user they will see an invitation the next time they log in on their dashboard. They can choose to accept or decline. Ivan will be notified of the user's decision. 
If the invitee is not a user of the app they will receive an email with a link inviting them to join the app. This invite will expire after a set amount of time (maybe 3 days). Ivan will be notified if either the invitation expires without being accepted or if the user accepts and joins the app and household.

### *Editing a household*

Eddy logs in to the app. He navigates to the Dashboard. As he is the owner of a household he can see an edit button next to the household details. He clicks on the edit button to edit, this opens an edit modal for the household. He can edit the name of the household. He can see a list of the household members and there is a delete button next to each user. He can also see the *Invite Member* button.

### *Deleting a household*

Derek logs in to the app. He navigates to the Dashboard. As he is the owner of a household he can see an edit button next to the household details. He clicks on the edit button to edit, this opens an edit modal for the household. He clicks on the delete button. He is presented with a modal dialog that asks him to confirm his intention to delete the household. He clicks continue. The modal closes and the household disappears from this profile page. It also disappears from all members profile pages as well.
